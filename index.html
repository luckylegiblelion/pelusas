Pelusas

flip · steal · stop before you poof

You’ll get a link to send your friend. 2–6 players.

You’ve been invited to a game!

How to play
  • On your turn, flip cards from the deck one at a time. They pile up in front of you.
  • When you flip a number that’s face-up in front of another player, you may steal every copy of it from everyone.
  • Stop whenever you like — but your cards stay face-up (and stealable!) until your next turn, when they bank safely.
  • Flip a number you already have face-up while holding 3+ cards and you go POOF — everything in front of you is swept out of the game.
  • Deck runs out → everyone banks. Highest total wins. Deck: eleven each of 1–5, seven each of 6–10.
